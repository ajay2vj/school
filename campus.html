Campus Infrastructure

  • Built up area of our school building is more than 20,000 Sq.ft.
  • Bright, spacious and ventilated class rooms.
  • Sports ground measuring 35,000 Sq.ft.
  • Spacious indoor auditorium measuring 3,000 Sq.ft.
  • 10 feet height compound wall all around the campus.
  • Fully furnished computer lab with 15 nos. of computers.
  • There are well equipped laboratories exclusively laboratory for Physics, Chemistry, Botany and Zoology
  • The school also houses special lab for Mathematics and Language lab for English and Hindi.
  • TV/DVD player and Projectors are installed in all the class rooms for audio visual learning system.
  • An excellent library with a vast collection of books is available for reference and reading for juniors and seniors.
